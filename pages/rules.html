📜 Easy Demon List Rules

1. Video Proof Required

Every completion submission must include a full, unedited video.


2. No Hacks or Cheats

Any hacked, botted, or cheated completions will be rejected.


3. Official Game Only

Records must be completed on the official version of Geometry Dash.


4. Respect Staff Decisions

The staff team has the final decision on rankings and submissions.


5. Be Respectful

Harassment, hate speech, or toxicity toward players or staff is not allowed.
